Latitude vs. Temperature

This graph displays how maximum temperature changes relative to the distance from the equator. As one moves from the equator, the maximum temperature decreases. As shown in the graph, this change is more dramatic in the northern hemisphere. This is likely due to the tilt of the earth's axis and its effect on the season. These data were obtained in January, which is summer in the southern hemisphere and temperatures are overall warmer. In contrast, at the time of data collection, it was winter in the Northern hemisphere and temperatures tend to be overall colder.
