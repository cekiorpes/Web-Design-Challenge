Latitude vs. Cloudiness

This graph displays the relationship between cloudiness and distance from the equator. This shows that the cloudiness at the time of data collection/processing was unaffected by the proximity or distance from the equator. There does not appear to be any differences between cities in the southern or northern hemispheres.
